# Business Requirements

## Executive Summary

KidSafe is a parental control phone solution targeting Indian families with children aged 5-14. The product combines a pre-configured Android phone with a parent companion app, enabling complete remote control and monitoring.

---

## Market Opportunity

### Target Market Size (India)

| Segment | Size | Source |
|---------|------|--------|
| Children 5-14 years | ~250 million | Census 2021 |
| Smartphone households | ~600 million | IAMAI 2024 |
| Parents concerned about screen time | 78% | Survey data |
| Addressable market (metro + T2) | ~50 million families | Estimate |
| Target penetration (Year 1) | 0.002% | 1,000 devices |

### Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Market | Price | Gap |
|------------|--------|-------|-----|
| Google Family Link | Global | Free | Software only, bypassable |
| Bark Phone | US | $49-79/mo | Not available in India |
| Gabb Phone | US | ~$100/phone + $25/mo | Not available in India |
| Pinwheel | US | $14.99/mo | Not available in India |

**Our Advantage**: No India-focused solution exists. Local apps, local payment, local support, competitive pricing.

## Risk Assessment

### Business Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Low initial demand | Medium | High | Pivot to B2B (schools) |
| High CAC | Medium | Medium | Focus on referrals |
| Price sensitivity | Medium | Medium | Offer annual discounts |
| Competition enters | Low | High | Build brand loyalty early |
| Cash flow issues | Medium | High | Pre-orders, phased inventory |

### Technical Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Samsung firmware update breaks DPC | Medium | High | Test before update, rollback |
| Firebase costs spike | Low | Medium | Cost monitoring, optimization |
| Play Store rejects app | Medium | Medium | Follow guidelines, appeal process |
| Security breach | Low | Critical | Security audit, encryption |

### Operational Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Supply chain delay | Medium | Medium | Buffer inventory |
| Support overwhelmed | Medium | Medium | Hire early, build FAQ |
| Key person dependency | High | High | Document everything |
